Garage

 

If you think working in the garage heavy and tedious, Garage will change your opinion.You will be able to plunge into work with parts of the vehicle, finding spare parts and tools. If you like the car theme - this machine is for you. Garage from Novomatic will not leave anyone indifferent.The game runs on 9 lines and accompanied by colorful graphics and great music. The maximum bet in the game is 25 credits, the game doubles the risk of winning. If during the game credits are over, the game stops. During the game, you can open the special combinations for open bonus games in which your winning amount will be much higher. Such games are represented by two. The essence of the first of these is to win a card from one of the dealers. The second bonus game, more unusual, it is necessary to open all the locks in the garage. Be careful, the error stops the bonus game. Garage game machine also has an extra level. Look forward to a tough choice "right" boxes, opening the box with the guard you lose the prize.
